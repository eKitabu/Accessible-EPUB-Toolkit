Marion’s best day

 

On Sunday, Marion got up early.

She walked to the window.

It was a sunny day.

 

Marion had a plan

She was going to Nana’s birthday party.

She wanted to put on a nice dress.

 

She took out her red dress.

Then she looked for her black shoes.

She also looked for her green socks.

 

Now Marion had black shoes, a red dress and green socks.

She knew she would look good in them.

She smiled.

 

“Oh! It may get cold later.

I need a jacket,” Marion said.

She went to look for her yellow jacket.

 

Marion got the yellow jacket.

It had a missing button.

She looked for her white sweater.

It was missing.

 

Marion looked for any pullover from the box.

She found her brown pullover.

The pullover looked nice.

 

Marion put on the red dress,
green socks and black shoes.
She also put on her brown
pullover.

She was very happy.

“I look great now,” she said.
