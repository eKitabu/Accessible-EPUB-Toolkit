Mata and I

This is my sister.

Her name is Mata and I am Pat.

My sister and I go to school.

The school is near our home.

After school we go home.

We play with our little brother.

His name is Bob.

My mother wants to give Bob food.

Bob sleeps a lot.

I play with Mata.

Father comes home.

He brings me a new book.

“Mata! Mata! See my book!” I say.

Bob wakes up.

Mother comes to sit with us.

I play with Father, Mother, Mata and Bob.
